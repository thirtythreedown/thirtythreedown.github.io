[DSGN] Nation of Makers coin holder

tl;dr: A 3D-printed holder to keep challenge coins on your keychain.

Bee went to NoMCON 2019 in Chattanooga and got a challenge coin as a souvenir.
When he came back, we designed a 3D-printed holder so he could keep it on his keychain.

  • Download the .STL files from the Github repository
  • Load the .STL Files in your favorite slicer: 1 of the edge holder and 4 of the coin holder claws
  • Drop your NoMCON challenge coin in the center of the coin holder.
  • Slide a holder claws on each peg of the edge holder for testing.
  • Secure each claw with a drop of krazy glue "just in case".
  • You're done!
