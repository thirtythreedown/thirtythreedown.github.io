[HARD] Keyes 3W LED module


tl;dr: Em picked up a box of Inland Arduino sensors a while back: they came pretty much without any documentation. She's been doing some research for a while and wanted to share, one module at a time. We hope it helps!

Pin connections
  • + goes to your Arduino +5V pin;
  • G goes to your Arduino Ground pin;
  • S goes to an Arduino digital pin;

And that's it! Once wired in, you can use this LED module just like a basic LED - just much much brighter. Don't stare at it directly!
